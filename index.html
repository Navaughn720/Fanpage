Biography

Scott Ramon Seguro Mescudi, born January 30, 1984, better known by his stage name, Kid Cudi, is an American rapper, singer, guitarist, songwriter, record producer, actor, and record executive.

He has widely been recognized as an influence on several contemporary hip hop and alternative acts.
